Sleep Analysis App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sleep Analysis App ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our sleep analysis application.

Data Collection

We collect the following data through your Oura Ring integration:

  • Sleep metrics and patterns
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Temperature data
  • Respiratory rate
  • Activity and readiness scores

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Analyzing your sleep patterns
  • Generating personalized sleep insights
  • Providing sleep quality visualizations
  • Calculating sleep trend analysis

Data Storage

We do not store your Oura Ring data permanently. Data is processed in real-time and is only temporarily cached during your active session. Your personal access token is stored locally on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All analysis is performed locally within the application.

Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Local data processing
  • No permanent data storage
  • Token-based authentication

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Disconnect your Oura Ring integration at any time
  • Request deletion of any cached data
  • Opt-out of data analysis

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
